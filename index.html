The Scanista Method™

The Scanista Method™ is a structured, neutral facilitation framework to clarify misalignment, de-escalate communication, and guide informed next steps.

Core Pillars:

  • Structured Intake: Gather essential information, prior attempts, and constraints.
  • Neutral Facilitation: Practitioner guides without advocating or diagnosing.
  • Decision Mapping: Explore options, trade-offs, and consequences.
  • Accountability & Closure: Documented takeaways and next steps.

Boundaries: Not therapy, legal, or medical advice.
